Want to reduce your hypertension risk? Here are a couple of suggestions:

1) Be active - daily physical activity = -20% risk
2) Eat fish - 4+ times fatty fish per week = -60% risk

You want to increase, not decrease it? Well that's easier: Get obese or start smoking and you are in for a +360% increased risk of hypertension...

... at least if your body works similar to that of the 1410 participants of a recent study from Macau. www-suppversity.com

Reference: Ke et al. Modifiable risk factors including sunlight exposure and fish consumption are associated with risk of hypertension in a large representative population from Macau. Ann Nutr Metab. In Press, Accepted Manuscript, Available online 1 November 2013

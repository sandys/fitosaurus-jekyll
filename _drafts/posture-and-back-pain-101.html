The Functional Movement Screen (FMS), developed by Gray Cook, MSPT, OCS, CSCS, of Danville, Va., is a screening method to identify biomechanical and functional issues in patients and athletes. The active straight leg raise tests the ability to perform separate movement of the lower extremity while maintaining stability in the torso

Most back problems stem not from the back, but because of years of  incorrect posture - which have impacted your thigh and hip muscles. The good news is that it can be corrected.
